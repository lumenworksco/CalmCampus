Our Pledges

Six commitments.
No small print.

These are not aspirations. They are hard constraints built into the architecture, the business model, and the team culture of CalmCampus.

🔒

Privacy by architecture

Your mental health data never leaves your device — not because of a policy, but because the architecture makes it impossible.

  • Zero cloud storage of behavioral data
  • On-device ML — no server roundtrips for health signals
  • No analytics SDKs, trackers, or ad identifiers
  • No account required — the app works fully offline
  • GDPR-native: nothing to regulate because nothing is collected
🧠

Evidence-based care only

Every intervention in CalmCampus is grounded in peer-reviewed clinical research. We do not ship wellness features without clinical backing.

  • All exercises sourced from CBT and ACT literature
  • No pseudoscience, no unvalidated "biohacking" content
  • Crisis resources link to accredited student counselling services
  • Anomaly detection calibrated per-student — no population averages
🎓

Free for students, always

Burnout affects every student regardless of income. The core experience of CalmCampus will never be paywalled.

  • Core wellness monitoring: permanently free
  • All intervention exercises: permanently free
  • No dark patterns, no premium nagging, no countdown timers
  • University licensing funds development — not user subscriptions
🔓

Transparent & open

We believe students and institutions deserve to understand exactly how CalmCampus works — and verify it.

  • Source code is open on GitHub under the MIT license
  • Wellness scoring algorithm is documented and auditable
  • Annual impact report (this page) published every year
  • No black-box AI: every alert explains why it was triggered
♿

Accessible by default

Burnout does not discriminate. CalmCampus must work for every student, including those who rely on assistive technology.

  • Full VoiceOver and TalkBack support across all screens
  • Dynamic Type and font scaling respected throughout
  • Minimum 4.5:1 WCAG AA contrast on all interactive elements
  • All micro-interventions available in text, audio, and haptic form
🚫

No engagement traps

Most wellness apps are designed to be opened daily. We are designed to be needed less over time — and we measure success that way.

  • No infinite feeds, no social comparison, no leaderboards
  • Notification frequency chosen by the student, never the algorithm
  • Streak mechanics exist to build habits, not to guilt users
  • Success metric: reduction in anomaly frequency over time
